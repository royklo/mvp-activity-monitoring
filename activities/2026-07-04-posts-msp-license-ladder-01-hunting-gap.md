The MSP License Ladder #1: The Hunting Gap

## Description
This blog post explores the limitations of basic Defender alerts provided by Microsoft 365 Business Premium and M365 E3 licenses for Managed Service Providers (MSPs). It highlights the benefits of upgrading to Defender for Endpoint Plan 2, which grants access to 30 days of KQL-queryable telemetry for advanced threat hunting. The post also includes a multi-tenant hunting script and a detailed license cost comparison to help MSPs determine the financial viability of the upgrade.

The content provides actionable insights for IT professionals and MSPs looking to enhance their security posture. By leveraging advanced hunting capabilities, readers can proactively investigate threats and improve their incident response strategies, ultimately delivering better security outcomes for their clients.
