## Description

It introduces the Intune Suite as a solution to address these gaps and discusses upcoming changes in licensing, including the inclusion of certain Intune Suite features in E3 and E5 plans starting July 2026, while noting the exclusion of Business Premium.

The content provides valuable insights for IT professionals and decision-makers managing endpoint security and device compliance. It helps organizations understand the evolving capabilities of Microsoft Intune and make informed decisions about licensing and feature adoption to enhance their endpoint management strategies.
