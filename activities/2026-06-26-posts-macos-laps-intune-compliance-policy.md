## Title
macOS LAPS vs. Intune Password Compliance Policy: Why It Breaks

## Description
This blog post investigates a recurring issue where macOS devices managed by Intune reject the correct LAPS-managed admin password due to compliance policy enforcement. It explains how password settings in Intune compliance policies trigger Open Directory to require a password change, even for newly rotated LAPS accounts, and details the technical root cause, diagnostic steps, and current workarounds.

The content is valuable for IT professionals and technical decision makers managing macOS devices with Intune, helping them understand and resolve authentication failures caused by compliance policy side effects. It clarifies the distinction between policy enforcement and auditing, reducing troubleshooting time and preventing unnecessary escalations.

## Private Description
Created to address a gap in Microsoft and Apple documentation regarding macOS LAPS and Intune compliance policy interactions. The post was triggered by repeated customer incidents and community reports where admins could not authenticate with the managed admin account, despite having the correct password. It aims to clarify the technical cause and provide actionable guidance.
